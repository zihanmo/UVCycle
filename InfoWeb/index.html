UV Cycle

Wearable tech for bike riders

Why choose us

Health

We care

In recent years, with high UV index value in Queensland, there is more potential for individuals to get sunburn or even skin cancer due to the lack of proper skin protection. With our project, you can not only take measures for protecting your skin by viewing the real-time UV index during your workout, but also, keep track of what is going on with your skin while cycling.

Experience

We care

As a bike rider, the main thing they would consider is the riding experience. In order to minimise the extra redundant weight added on the bike and improve the experience by providing useful UV information, only a small waterproof device (The UV Ring) will be directly attached on the bike handle and seen by the users. In this way, cyclists can choose to have break if they don’t want to expose under the sun for a long time, and also have no extra weight for the riding experience.
